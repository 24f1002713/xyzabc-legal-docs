Zync Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

Welcome to Zync ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Zync (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Email address - For account identification and communication
  • Display name - To personalize your experience
  • Google ID - For secure authentication

1.2 Profile Information

You voluntarily provide profile information including:

  • Profile name and bio
  • Social media links (Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, etc.)
  • Interests and "seeking" categories
  • Contact information you choose to share

1.3 Bluetooth Data

Zync uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology to:

  • Broadcast your profile to nearby users
  • Discover profiles of users in your proximity
  • Enable offline, peer-to-peer profile sharing
Note: BLE advertising data is transmitted locally between devices and is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly use the global search feature.

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • App interaction data (features used, screens visited)
  • Search queries (for global discovery feature)
  • Subscription and purchase history
  • Device information (for troubleshooting)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Enable profile discovery via BLE and global search
  • Process premium subscriptions and payments
  • Send important notifications about matches and nearby users
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information is shared with other Zync users through:

  • BLE broadcasting when enabled
  • Global search results (if you appear in searches)

3.2 With Service Providers

We do not sell user data to third parties.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or government request.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure token-based authentication (JWT)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring
Data Retention: Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Your Rights & Choices

5.1 Access & Control

You have the right to:

  • View your profile and stored data within the App
  • Edit your profile information at any time
  • Control BLE broadcasting and scanning settings
  • Manage notification preferences
